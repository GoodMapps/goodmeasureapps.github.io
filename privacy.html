Privacy Policy

Last updated:

GoodMeasureApps (“we”, “our”, “us”) builds educational apps including MentorMe. We respect your privacy and design our apps to collect the minimum data necessary to function.

Information We Collect

  • Account basics (optional): name or nickname, email for login/reset.
  • Progress data: completed modules, quiz results, streaks.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and basic analytics to improve stability.

We do not collect or share precise location, contact lists, microphone recordings, or photo libraries. MentorMe has no peer-to-peer chat.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core features (modules, quizzes, progress)
  • Improve app reliability and content
  • Communicate important updates (e.g., password reset)

Gift Card Promotions

From time to time, we may run optional engagement drawings for gift cards. No purchase is necessary. These promotions are not gambling and do not use cryptocurrency or NFTs.

Children & Teens

MentorMe is designed for teens with parent-friendly controls. We avoid collecting personal data beyond what’s needed for educational progress. Parents or guardians can contact us to review or delete a teen’s account data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Limited trusted processors may handle analytics or crash diagnostics strictly to support app functionality.

Data Security & Retention

We use industry-standard safeguards. We retain data only as long as necessary for the app’s purpose or as required by law.
